Illegal Mining Monitoring

The project focused on tracking illegal mining in Ghana.
Here, locations where illegal mining takes place is recorded,
and hotspots targetted using satellite images.
This aimed at helping African security agencies and mining
authorities understand and detect illegal mining better.
